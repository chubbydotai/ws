What problem exists?

Housing Crisis

750,000 to 1 million homeless humans in the USA on a given night. USA market short 4 million homes in 2024.

Manufacturing Gap

Major push for domestic USA manufacturing as AI/humanoid robotics race with China accelerates.

Global Infrastructure

2 billion humans without adequate toilets and clean water. 2 billion more people in next 25 years.

Education Gap

Half as many post-secondary institutions as school districts. No major push for higher education at scale.

What is the Chubby.Ai solution?

Chubby.MOM: A network of 1,000 Chubby Centers — 1MW EDGE Factory-Datacenters, allowing us to seed 1,000 one-hundred to two-thousand acre Chubby Towns — Factory College Towns around the USA.

Core Values

Maslow's Hierarchy

  • Physiological: air, water, food, shelter, sleep, clothing, reproduction
  • Safety: personal security, employment, resources, health, prosperity
  • Love & Belonging: friendship, intimacy, family, sense of connection
  • Esteem: respect, self-esteem, status, recognition, strength, freedom
  • Self-Actualization: desire to become the most that one can be

Tony Robbins 6 Human Needs

  • Live Happy: Certainty
  • Work with Passion: Significance
  • Play Hard: Uncertainty/Variety
  • Love Harder: Love/Connection
  • Learn & Grow: Growth
  • Pay-it-Forward: Contribution

Why Chubby.Ai?

We're not just building technology—we're building a future where basic human needs are met, communities are revitalized, and people can reach their full potential.

Real Problems, Real Solutions

We're addressing the housing crisis, manufacturing gaps, and infrastructure challenges head-on. Chubby.MOM and Chubby.Town aren't concepts—they're concrete plans to build 1 million homes and 1,000 Factory-Datacenters by 2030.

Walking the Talk
